Our story

A kitchen in a garden, not a garden on a plate

Verde began as a Sunday lunch table under the pepper tree at the end of our vegetable rows. Seven years later the table is longer, the fire is bigger, and the idea is unchanged: cook what the garden gives, the day it gives it.

Chef Nandi Dlamini leads a kitchen of six, working from a single wood-fired hearth. No freezers, no fryers — just seasons.
