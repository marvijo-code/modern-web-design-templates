About

The short version

Design, for me, is editing — removing everything that distracts from the one thing worth saying.

I studied typography in Johannesburg and cut my teeth in editorial studios in Amsterdam before going independent in 2019. Since then I've partnered with over forty clients, from single-founder startups to national museums.

My practice is deliberately small: one project at a time, involved from strategy through launch. If a project needs motion, code or photography, I bring in trusted collaborators.
